How much does starting a business really cost

This webinar provides NSW businesses with information about the costs that need to be considered when starting a new business.

Webinar for NSW businesses

  • Cost: Free
  • Location: Online
  • Date: Saturday 18
  • Time: 10:00 - 11:30 AM AEST

So you’ve got a great idea, you’ve got the know how, and you’re ready to break out on your own and start your new venture. Whilst it’s fun thinking about what your business cards will look like and what font you’ll use on your website, have you really thought about what else you’ll need to pay for?

In this online workshop, you will learn about things that you will need to consider when working out how much will starting a business will really cost you – from what corporate structure you choose, getting legal and accounting advice and working out what sales you need to make to break even. The webinar will run through the things that require essential planning to make your new business a viable one that will be around for the long term.

After this webinar, you will have learnt:

  • how to plan your business for long term sustainability
  • which templates to use when it comes to planning the numbers for your new business
  • how to identify the areas where you can scrimp on costs and the areas you need to splurge.

PLEASE NOTE: This webinar is specifically tailored for New South Wales businesses.
