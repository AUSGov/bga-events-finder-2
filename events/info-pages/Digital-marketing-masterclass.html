Does your business use social media? Are you ready to refine your skills on the social media platforms to get better results?

This is not a beginner class. Do you already understand one or all of the major platforms from Facebook, Snapchat, LinkedIn and Instagram? We will run through setting up paid media and learn tips and tricks to make them more effective.

Digital Marketing Masterclass will teach you the secrets that the digital marketing industry use to gain results for their clients on each platform. Learn what tools they use which can either save you time or money in your own business.

There is a maximum of 20 spots available. This is a live online event and will be recorded. Those who attend will receive a copy of the recording and any available resources.
