First Wednesday Connect is a regular monthly get-together aiming to energise, share stories and encourage collaboration between entrepreneurs, researchers, investors and innovators of all sorts.

Hear from Canberra’s budding entrepreneurs in our high intensity 60 second pitching format and meet new people over drinks and nibbles.

We are following the ACT Government’s advice on events and will have a very limited capacity. Please ensure you have the Check In CBR app installed.
